    Why Belize?

    The Belize Project is a ministry that serves other ministries efforts, NGO and NPO in Belize that are like minded in our Mission. Our Mission statement is loving people , places and things to Life through Jesus Christ. The Belize Project engages in many projects supporting spiritual goodwill efforts, directly engages in rehabilitation and reintegration, Education and youth intervention and Economic development and entrepreneurship.

    We support in diverse ways: materials, Prayer, Resources, finance, mentoring, trainings, encouragement and much more! The vision of The Belize Project is to find those in Belize who are making a difference and we walk alongside them. Our Ministry focus is on Rehabilitation farms, Rehabilitation 1/2 houses, Safe homes, prisons, schools, churches, education, Youth intervention, micro lending, to name a few.

    We are bringing awareness and partnering with others to cultivate more Healing communities in Belize.
