Hope Gala is an event to be held in Belize. This event serves in promoting the work The Belize Project does through it's different ministries such as, Jacob's Rehabilitation Farm Center, House of Dorcas, Journey to Freedom among some others. It also seeks to be a fundraiser event which will help all these different ministries in whatever way possible.

All donations gathered through this fundraiser will be of great benefit to each of our ministries.
They will be divided according to the ministry needs.
