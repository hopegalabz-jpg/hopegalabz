The 2026 Hope Gala

An Evening of Purpose

The Hope Gala is a premier fundraising event held in Belize. This elegant evening serves to promote the transformative work The Belize Project does throughout the country alongside our dedicated ministry partners.

More than just a gathering, it is a vital fundraiser. The generous contributions gathered during this event allow us to continue providing resources, education, and rehabilitation to those who need it most, giving them a second chance and a hope for a new tomorrow.

How Your Giving Helps

Transparency and stewardship are at the core of what we do. All donations gathered through this fundraiser will be of great benefit to each of our partner ministries.

Funds are carefully divided and allocated according to specific, pressing ministry needs—whether that is funding Journey to Freedom curriculums, supporting the rehabilitation farm, or providing safe shelter for vulnerable women.
