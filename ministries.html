Jacob's Rehabilitation Farm Center

Jacob's Rehabilitatiion Farm Center is a Non-Profit Organization that brings hope, recovery and restoration to men. Its mission is to assist men who are suffering from drug and alcohol addiction. We have a core curriculum that we teach weekly and then many additional courses which we teach often. The core courses are Journey to Freedom, Alcoholics Anonymous 12 Steps, Teen Challenge, and The Science of Addiction. Some of the additional courses cover topics like Personal Financial, How to be a good father and husband? and How to treat women respectfully. It provides a therapeutic six- month residential program and assists them to reintegrate into society as productive citizens in their communities giving them a second chance and a hope for a new tomorrow.

House of Dorcas

The House of Dorcas is a Christian Organization dedicated to assist women in Domestic Violence and Human Trafficking situations. Our main goal is to provide vulnerable victims a safe place for them to spend time while their case is reviewed and addressed. Our ministry also assists women through Journey to Freedom and other educational programs that will equip them to become a productive force in our society.

Restore Small Groups

Restore Small Groups helps us deal with life controlling issues and guides us through a life transforming process. Our mission is to be a healing community supporting each individual's unique path of transformation and well-being. One of our main focus has been Kolbe Prison where we lead Journey to Freedom sessions and training. Over 1800 men and women have gone through their life transforming process and we hope we can reach out to as many as we can. Other ministries served through Journey to Freedom are: Jacob's Rehabilitation Farm Center, Youth Hostel Foundation, Tubal Vocational School, Excelsior High School, Sadie Vernon High School, to name a few. Restore small groups has been and will continue to be committed to serve and support anyone desiring a change in his/her life.
