Jacob's Rehabilitation Farm Center

Jacob's Rehabilitation Farm Center is a Non-Profit Organization that brings hope, recovery, and restoration to men. Its mission is to assist men who are suffering from drug and alcohol addiction. We have a core curriculum that we teach weekly and then many additional courses which we teach often.

The core courses are Journey to Freedom, Alcoholics Anonymous 12 Steps, Teen Challenge, and The Science of Addiction. Some of the additional courses cover topics like Personal Finance, How to be a good father and husband, and How to treat women respectfully. It provides a therapeutic six-month residential program and assists them to reintegrate into society as productive citizens in their communities, giving them a second chance and a hope for a new tomorrow.

House of Dorcas

The House of Dorcas is a Christian Organization dedicated to assisting women in Domestic Violence and Human Trafficking situations. Our main goal is to provide vulnerable victims a safe place for them to spend time while their case is reviewed and addressed. Our ministry also assists women through Journey to Freedom and other educational programs that will equip them to become a productive force in our society.

Restore Small Groups

Restore Small Groups helps us deal with life-controlling issues and guides us through a life-transforming process. Our mission is to be a healing community supporting each individual's unique path of transformation and well-being. One of our main focuses has been Kolbe Prison where we lead Journey to Freedom sessions and training. Over 1800 men and women have gone through their life-transforming process and we hope we can reach out to as many as we can.

Other ministries served through Journey to Freedom are: Jacob's Rehabilitation Farm Center, Youth Hostel Foundation, Tubal Vocational School, Excelsior High School, Sadie Vernon High School, to name a few. Restore Small Groups has been and will continue to be committed to serving and supporting anyone desiring a change in his/her life.

Believe in Them Ministry

Meet God Halfway Rehabilitation & Recovery

The Believe in Them Ministry, Meet God Halfway Rehabilitation & Recovery Program exists to guide incarcerated men through a process of spiritual renewal, personal accountability, and behavioral transformation. Its mission is to provide participants with a structured environment where they can reflect on their past choices, confront destructive habits, and develop the spiritual and practical tools necessary to rebuild their lives.

Through faith-based guidance, mentorship, and group discussion, the program equips these men for a productive, responsible, and purpose-driven future both inside the facility and upon their return to society.

Kolbe Foundation

Through our Journey to Freedom program at the Kolbe Foundation, countless lives have been transformed. More than 7,000 men and women have successfully graduated after completing a life-changing process that restores hope beyond their past mistakes. Participants learn to love, forgive, believe in themselves, and embrace the truth that lasting change is possible. Each graduate leaves with renewed purpose, restored dignity, and the hope of a brighter future.

Hope Center Belize

Hope Center Belize is a faith-based ministry committed to helping women find hope, healing, and a renewed sense of purpose. Through a caring and supportive environment, the center walks alongside women as they overcome life's challenges, grow in their faith, and build a stronger foundation for the future. The Journey to Freedom program has provided a meaningful path for personal healing and transformation, equipping participants to work through past hurts, embrace their identity, develop healthy relationships, and move forward with confidence, freedom, and hope.

Flourish 31

At the heart of Flourish 31 is the belief that every person is created in the image of God and reflects the creativity of the Creator. Their mission is rooted in serving God by loving and serving others, honoring Him in everything they do. Through a commitment to creativity, sustainability, and community, Flourish 31 empowers individuals and families to achieve greater economic independence, strengthen their communities, and care for the environment. By creating opportunities for personal growth, meaningful employment, and responsible stewardship, they are helping build stronger families, healthier communities, and a more hopeful future.

Our Programs

Education & Youth Intervention

Journey to Freedom

The Belize Project celebrates the many success stories of individuals who have completed programs through Jacob's Rehabilitation Farm Center, secondary schools, and the House of Dorcas. Graduates of Journey to Freedom, Alcoholics Anonymous, Teen Challenge, and The Science of Addiction are rebuilding their lives and successfully reintegrating into society. Their transformed lives stand as powerful testimonies that lasting change is possible through faith, perseverance, and support. As beacons of hope in their families and communities, they demonstrate that, through Christ, lives can be restored and futures can be renewed.

The Belize Project also partners with local schools by providing educational resources, training, and support for both teachers and students. Through the Wilson Reading Program, we are helping improve literacy and strengthen foundational reading skills. Our Youth Journey to Freedom program is delivered in secondary schools, including the Tubal Trade and Vocational Institute, Maud Williams High School, Excelsior High School, and Sadie Vernon Technical High School, equipping students with practical life skills, character development, and hope for the future. In addition, our Journey team trains and annually certifies students, teachers, and counselors to effectively mentor and support individuals facing life's challenges. Together, these initiatives are helping build stronger schools, healthier communities, and brighter futures for the next generation.

Economic Development & Entrepreneurship

The Belize Project supports men and women as they launch and grow small businesses by providing financial, material, and spiritual assistance. Through this initiative, we help individuals build sustainable livelihoods, achieve financial independence, and create brighter futures for themselves and their families. We currently support a variety of micro-enterprises, including an upholstery business, a tennis shoe business, and a jewelry and craft business. By investing in entrepreneurship, we are helping transform lives, strengthen communities, and create lasting opportunities for success.
